Take Turns is a website that sorts out you day to day chores. Aren't you tired of reminding your friend , roommate or anyone you live with that it's there turn to do the room's chores, like throwing trash away or cleaning the room or any other task. Our website solves this problem of yours and you can simply login for free and register your roommates, and it will simply use an algorithm to show who's turn it is to do the chores. Sitback, relax and clear your mind of these things and let us handle this for you.
